January 1st 8h-??

‚òü Scroll for info and location ‚òü

Hijinx crew is excited to welcome Davis Galvin, all the way from Pittsburgh, for our third annual New Year's Day brunch. Whether you're celebrating a new year of self-growth, the last day of Hanukkah, or the circumcision of Jesus Christ, we invite you spend the first day of 2025 dancing and relaxing at the coziest loft in town.

Everyone arriving will be greeted with a caesar (while supplies last) to toast a new year. We will be selling drinks and hanukkah-themed brunch dishes to sate your appetites. And for the first time a second space will be opening up at La 30, a chillout room accessible via the terrasse, where guests are welcome to snooze off their big night to ambient tunes.
