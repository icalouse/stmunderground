Important info for the OVERGROUND Fundraiser

Read through all Health & Safety guidelines before seeing location ☟

Masks are required on the dancefloor; please maintain distance while dancing

Hand sanitizer will be available at the info table

Please bring water bottles - We will have some water available for refill, and some labelled cups for people to use during the event

Please bring hats and sunscreen - There is limited shade in the park

In order for the event to stay running, social distancing rules must be respected - We can’t give the cops any excuse to shut us down!

For the second year in a row we are supporting Head & Hands. We are asking for $20 from every attendee to help this wonderful organization to continue to provide for the community

More about Head & Hands (scroll☟)

  • H&H provides legal, medical, and social services to marginalized youth in Montreal
  • These services include free weekly drop-in medical clinics, legal information and consultations, counselling, a young parents’ program, youth drop-in, tutoring, street workers, and the Sense Project peer-based sex ed in high schools
  • Currently, they are running an at-home project fund, the aim being to recognize and compensate (QT)(BIPOC) youth for their expertise (lived experience) that is not necessarily cultivated in an academic or professional setting

To make your donation, please send an Interac e-transfer to beaubien365@gmail.com (autodeposit enabled)

To maintain full transparency, all finances and donation receipts will be shared here after the event

You must agree to respect the safety guidelines to see the location of this event (wear a mask!)

Or use Google Maps to direct you. But we recommend crossing the 40 at Iberville!

About

STM Underground is a series of raves in Montreal originally organized by Maurijn Rezvani, Celia Robinovitch, and Nicolas Levy

Although COVID-19 has put a halt to physical raves, stay tuned for streaming parties, and eventually, outdoor parties

We are in no way affiliated with the Société de Transport de Montréal, although we endorse them and hope you enjoy the ride :)

Big thanks to Emma Forgues for background design and Matthew Halpenny for streaming configuration
